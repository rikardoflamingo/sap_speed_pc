Powerful Features

A summary of some of Site App Pro’s features..
Hazard & Control Measures

Create your health and safety forms in just a few steps. Select from a list of hazards or add your own custom hazards.

Task Scheduling

Take out the guesswork by scheduling daily, monthly, or yearly tasks so you and your team always know exactly what tasks need to be completed next.

Multilingual Support

We understand the world is a diverse place and the building industry is as multicultural as it gets. Our platform currently supports English, Mandarin and Korean, with more languages on the way.

Custom Form Builder

Use our standard form templates or create your own forms from scratch to suit your business. All plans have unlimited forms so you can create as many forms as you need.
New Feature: Now supports Quiz type questions.

PIN Signing

Traceability is is key when trying to work out who did what and when. Our PIN and digital signature makes signing forms quick and easy.

Management Dashboard

As a business owner or manager, you need visibility of what is going on in your business. That's where the Dashboard comes in with all the info and metrics you need in one place.

Business Insights

Site App Pro’s powerful reports give you full visibility of what is happening (or not happening) in your business.

Photo & Video Capture

"A picture says a thousand words". You can add photos and videos to your answers. Great for capturing hazards, notes or something that needs maintenance.
‍New Feature: Now supports QR code scanning.

Powerful Reporting

Reporting is a key feature of any compliance platform and Site App Pro is no exception. We have a suite of out of the box reports.

Unlimited Staff

We know that workers like to move around so we don't charge you for the number of staff you have signing forms. All staff have their own PIN for trace ability and to make the digital form signing quick and easy.

Encrypted Data Storage

Data privacy is a big deal. Your data is proprietary to you and we take your data storage seriously. All of our data is encrypted and backed up using Amazon Web Services (AWS).

Pay Month By Month

Our payment plans are simple, easy to understand and above all affordable.If your requirements are complex talk to our sales team about a custom pricing plan designed to suit your business needs.
